About Us

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for people in Saudi Arabia and beyond by providing innovative, high-quality, and affordable pharmaceutical products while maintaining the highest standards of ethics and integrity in all our operations.

Our Vision

To be the leading pharmaceutical company in the Middle East, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and improving healthcare outcomes for all communities we serve.

Our History

2005

Cosmos Pharma was founded in Riyadh with a focus on importing and distributing essential medications to meet the growing healthcare needs in Saudi Arabia.

2010

Expanded operations to include partnerships with international pharmaceutical companies, bringing innovative treatments to the Saudi market.

2015

Established our state-of-the-art research and development facility, focusing on developing medications tailored to regional health needs.

2020

Launched our first locally manufactured product line, marking a significant milestone in our journey toward pharmaceutical independence.

2025

Celebrating 20 years of excellence with expanded facilities, new international partnerships, and a growing portfolio of innovative healthcare solutions.
